MealPicker

Can't think of what to cook? Mealpicker will help you pick a meal based on what you have. Search up any food stuff at home and it will give you a long list of meals that has that particular ingredent in it. Interesting right? It also goes on to providing you with the recipe and also a vidoe link for those who prefer watching a video. This feature can be used by recipe applications and websites. Also resturants, health and fitness centers in order to find meals suitable for cetain people. This is one of my first projects. It was built with HTML, CSS and Javascript. I built it when I was learning how to work with complex APIs.

Jobify

Looking for a Job? Look no more! Got to Jobify now and find job postings from the biggest tech companies in the world. You can search for different job types, location and also by company. I was also excited to work on this because it can be used in the real world.
